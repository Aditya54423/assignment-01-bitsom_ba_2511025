## Database Recommendation 
When designing a patient management system for a healthcare startup,I would recommend using MySQL as the primary database.Healthcare systems handle highly sensitive and critical data such as patient records,medical history,prescriptions and blling information . In such environments , maintaining strong data consistencey and data integrity is essential.MySQL follows the ACID properties i.e Atomicity,Consistency,Isolation,Durability,which ensures that trasactions are reliable and that data remains accurate even during system failures.Like when updating a patient's treatment or billing information ,the system must gurantee that transaction happens completely or not at all.

Where as MongoDB and other NoSQL databases follow BASE approach i.e (Basically A,Eventual consistencyvailable,Soft State).While this model offers higher scalability and flexibility ,it may allow temporary inconsistencies in the data.For healthcare systems where incorrect or incinsistent patient records could lead to serious consequences and eventual consistency might not be acceptable.

The CAP theorem also supports choosing MySQL for this use case as in distributed systems,only two of three guarantees among Consistency,Avalibility and Partition tolerance can be fullfilled at same time.Healthcare systems prioritize consistency over availibility,meaning it is crucial that the data remains correct than always accesible in case of network issues. and in case if the system also includes a fraud detection moule ,the architure might change slightly as Fraud detection often involves analyzing large volumes of semi-structured or streamiing data such as transaction logs and behavioural patterns . In that case , MongoDB and other NoSQL database could be used alongside MySQL for analystics and real-time detection tasks.
